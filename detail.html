FAQ

Why this trip?
Discovering places in the old town of Bangkok, while learning local stories of each place. Let me show you around ;)
How local is it?
Since we will be exploring places by Chao Phraya river, you will get ti observe the local way of life and try different street foods. :9
What makes it unique?
Not only visiting the most iconic landmarks of this are, I'll take you to try some of the most delicious Thai foods in this area. (because I'm a big fan of food. lol ) Plus, I'll teach you some Thai words too.
